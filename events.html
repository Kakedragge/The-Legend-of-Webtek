Date: 21.12.16.
Time: 18:00-21:00
Location: Trondheim Galleri

You are welcome to my upcoming exhibition at Trondheim Galleri this christmas, which takes place at Trondheim Galleri. This showcase consists of my ongoing winter project in landscape painting and photography with the overarching themes of isolation and loneliness. Entrance fee is 50 kr for people above the age of 13, otherwise free.
